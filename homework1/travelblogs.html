My Trip to Japan

On my trip to Japan, I discovered how effortlessly tradition and modernity blend together. One moment I was walking through serene Shinto shrines surrounded by ancient trees, and the next I was sipping matcha in a minimalist café overlooking neon-lit skyscrapers. I found hidden alleys with tiny ramen shops where locals greeted me warmly, and I learned that vending machines really do sell everything. But what surprised me most was how small, everyday gestures — a bow, a simple ‘arigatou gozaimasu’ — could make me feel so welcome in a culture so different from my own.
